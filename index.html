How it works?

The process for loading and processing data in the hub is straight forward to simple to understand.

Get Data

Where is your data? Login to the Information Hub and create a "data point" indicating where to get the data from, or start dropping data files.

More info Step 1

Describe the Data

Describing the data is about identifying natural keys, data that is important and needs to be tracked or data that is sensitive and needs to be encrypted. After this, use the one-click staging loader and you have a job that can be re-run indefinitely and preserve the data changes over time.

More info Step 2

Validate the Data

Validating data is specifying what the data should look like, and what action to take when it is invalid. This process is straight forward, and when you've defined a rule (such as the pattern for a phone number) you can reuse this across the rest of your data. When rules fail, you can opt to clean the value, store the record in a rejection table, or completely abandon the job and alert support staff.

More info Step 3

Transform the Data

Transforming is where you prepare your data into the form you need to analyse or deliver it to other systems. The platform is built with flexible functions that allow linking, standardising, aggregating, and time-series analysis. The end result is that you will have an automated job that will transform data ready for consumption.

More info Step 4

Publish and Deliver

Transformed data can be published where it is needed. This may beto data warehouse for reporting, to other applications, to your customers, or your analysts.
